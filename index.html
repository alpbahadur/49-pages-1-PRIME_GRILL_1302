Our Story

Welcome to PRIME GRILL 1302, where family tradition meets culinary excellence. Located in the vibrant neighborhood of Condado, San Juan, we bring together the finest hand-cut prime meats and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Our passion is simple: to provide an exceptional dining experience that combines the highest quality ingredients with genuine hospitality. Every dish is crafted with care, sourcing the best local ingredients Puerto Rico has to offer.

From our family to yours, we invite you to experience the perfect blend of premium steakhouse dining and welcoming family-style service.
